If you don't know where you are at every moment financially, you are lost. Let us make sure that your financials are always up to date, accurate and accessible. Logistics and Fulfillment

“Having the right item in the right quantity at the right time at the right place for the right price in the right condition to the right customer” is how Mallik sums up logistics. But if it were that simple, everyone could do it. In truth, managing logistics is one of the most challenging aspects of any businesses.

Whether you’re an established entity or just starting out, as a retailer you need to know exactly where your goods are at any given time, what the status of your outstanding shipments are, and how much and when to order next. Our experience and global connections make it easier to navigate the intricacies of logistics, as we work with our global partners to bring you access to affordable warehousing and fulfillment all around the world. You can grow your business as large as you want without investing in additional warehouses, personnel or infrastructure because Cresca makes it easier for you to scale your business. Warehouse management, transportation management, delivery management, asset management, and border procedure management are just a few of the areas where we provide exceptional value to our clients.

Payment Processing

Selling in a new country can be challenging. Consolidating payments across multiple channels can be a headache. Payment processing often involves excessive paperwork, compliance complexities, merchant processors, transacting banks and other parties - and everyone wants a cut of your margin for their "trouble." We can alleviate much of the burden associated with online payment processing for small and medium retailers.

Through one of our strategic partnerships, we're able to offer our customers the benefits of our excellently leveraged payment forwarding and consolidation services. The advantages of this service are particularly outstanding for international customers, who go through more challenges to get bank and PayPal accounts that can accept United States Dollars. By utilizing these payment forwarding services, our clients can tap the entire US market without having to sacrifice margin for outrageous international or conversion fees. And Cresca's role here is not to take a cut from your margins - heaven forbid! We will assess your current state of payment processing and learn what you're trying to accomplish.

If our partner can be the most strategic advocate to accomplish your pursuits, then we'll team you up - if not, we'll help guide you in the right direction to simplify your arrangement and maximize your profits.
